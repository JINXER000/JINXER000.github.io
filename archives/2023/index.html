My Detail CV.

I am a last-year Ph.D. student at Mechanical and Automation Engineering department, The Chinese University of Hong Kong, supervised by Prof. Ben M. Chen.

My research interests lies in developing intelligent robot planning and perception algorithms that enables real-world tasking. So far most of my work is done on mobile robots, e.g., UGVs and UAVs.
